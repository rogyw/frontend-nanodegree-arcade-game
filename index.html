How to Play

The Object of the game: Move the boy/girl up to the water supply - avoiding the bugs travelling along the pavement.

How to Play:To move, press and release the keyboard arrow keys (Up/Down/Left/Right). To restart, refresh the page.

Scoring: Each time the player reaches the water one point is earned and the player is safely transported back to the grass. If the player touches a bug they lose a life and restart from the grass.

Have fun!
